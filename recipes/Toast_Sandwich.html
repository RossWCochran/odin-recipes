Ladies and Gentlemen, I give you the Bread Sandwich

    For this recipe you will need:
  • Bread
  • Butter
  • salt (optional)
  • Peper (optional)
  1. Select 3 slices of bread. Each can be from the same loaf or different loaves if you are feeling a bit frisky.
  2. Select 1 slice and toast once at your preferred toaster setting. I recommend using the densest slice.
  3. When completed, butter each side and, should you want and extra kick, salt and/or pepper each side lightly(particularly if the butter is salted).
  4. Layer the bread and toast in the following order: bread, toast, bread.
  5. Done! Enjoy your flavorful and filling snack!
